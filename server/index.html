#dislike: Hashtag Dislike

The best way to show your dislike on Twitter.

How does it work?

Just reply to any tweet with #dislike somewhere in the reply.

How do I see how many others dislike a tweet?

Download the Chrome extension. It adds a new box in the detail view of a tweet showing how many others dislike that tweet.
